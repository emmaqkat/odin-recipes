The Soup of the Cabbage Beast

This soup is very tasty. And it contains at least 1 nutrient of some kind. I don't know, I'm just a website.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage (optional)
  • Onion type things
  • Some kind of fat
  • Garlic
  • Broth or Milk
  • Spices (Pepper, Dill, Paprika, etc.)

You Must Cook It

  1. Fry up the little onion and garlic bits until they are nice and yummy looking
  2. Chop the cabbage very fine
  3. Cook the cabbage in the oil with the garlic and onions
  4. Add your other spices
  5. When the cabbage is all soft and cooked, Add your broth
  6. Let it boil for a long long time
  7. Eat it with your mouth
